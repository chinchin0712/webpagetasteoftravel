Tom Yum Goong A spict Thai Delight

Tom Goong is a classic Thai soup renowned for it's bold flavor and aromatic spices. Taste of Travel, This hot and sour soup fetures a tantaliz, The key to a delicious Tom Yum Goong lies in the balance of flavor sweet, sour, salty, and spicy soup.

Ingredients

    •Lemongrass: 2 stalks •Thai basil: 1 cup •Kaffir Lime leaves: 3 leaves •Shrimp: 500g

Preparation:

  1. Simmer the broth for 10 minutes.
  2. Add lemongrass.
  3. Stir in shrimp and cook until pink.
  4. Season with the fish sauce, lime juice, chili paste.
  5. Garnish with Thai basil and serve hot.

Cooking tips

¼ cup of fish sauce adds authentic Thai flavor. tablespoon of chili paste gives the soup it's signature heat.
